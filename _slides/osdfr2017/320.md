### Procedure
- Create bootable cinder volume
- Attach volume to utility VM
- Export VM disk to utility VM
- Convert to raw:
  - VM: `qemu-img convert -O raw in.vmdk /dev/vdx`
  - Metal: `dd if=/dev/sdx | ssh UtilityVM "dd of=/dev/vdx"`
